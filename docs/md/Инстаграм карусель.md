### **Pro-Tips for Production**

1. **Image Treatment:** Apply a slight "Grain" filter to the photos to give them a gritty, editorial look. Increase contrast so the blacks in the photos melt perfectly into the `#0a0a0a` background.
    
2. **Gradient Direction:** Use a linear gradient at a 135° angle for the buttons and highlights to create a sense of movement.
    
3. **Spacing:** Ensure at least 100px of "breathable" margin on the left and right sides so the text feels premium and not cramped.
