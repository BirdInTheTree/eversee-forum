Behind EVERSEE

EVERSEE grew out of over 30 years of producing large-scale shows and immersive events — from Dinner-Show Palazzo in Germany to touring productions and major brand events for Mercedes-Benz, IBM, and DHL across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and Asia.

Since 2015, we've been running the creative industry forum (formerly Show Your Show) — bringing together the people who build the shows and the people who build the tools. The Creative Summit is our most focused format yet: a small, curated group working on real problems instead of watching presentations.
